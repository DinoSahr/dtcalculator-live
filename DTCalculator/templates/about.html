Our Story

Purpose of this app

Though not enforced legally, but the allowed percentage limit on tipping in the Us is between 15-20%. Where Fifteen percent is considered the minimum of a decent tip, and Twenty percent considered excellent. When your tip value is over this limit, some Financial Institutions would send you a confirmation email to confrim you did surely intended on giving the amount you'd given to your server or bartender as part of a tip for their service. And if you're me who usually get those confirmation email from your Financial Instution, then this app was built with you in mind. This app ensures that you're never over the maximum allowed tipping limits. Andy by now you'd probably be asking how?. Well all you need to do is enter total on your receipt you get from your server or bartender. Then select the tip percentage you'd like to give between Fifteen Percent and Tweenty Percent, and hit the submit button, the app would provide you with a tip amount base on the percentage you'd selected and the final total after the tip amount is applied. The app also provide you with a real time report of the amount you'd spent on tips and your all your all time grand total spending from going out to bars and restaurants.
